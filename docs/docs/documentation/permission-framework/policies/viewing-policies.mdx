# Viewing Policies

Understanding and managing policies is crucial for maintaining a robust access control system within the Zelthy platform. Here's how you can easily view policies using the App Panel interface:

## Accessing the Policy Page

1. **Navigate to Permission Management:**
   Begin by accessing the "Permission Management" menu within App Panel.

2. **Access the Policy Submenu:**
   Within the "Permission Management" menu, click on the "Policy" submenu.

3. **Policy Page Display:**
   Clicking on the "Policy" submenu will direct you to the Policy page. This page presents a comprehensive table view showcasing all existing policies within your application.

The Policy page provides an organized overview of all policies, enabling you to quickly review and manage the access control landscape of your healthcare applications.
